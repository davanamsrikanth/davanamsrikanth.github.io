This website consolidates my informational writings and perspectives on public administration, governance, and public sector institutions. The views expressed are personal and for general informational purposes.

About

Based in Hyderabad, India, Davanam Srikanth works at the intersection of public administration, indirect taxation, and governance. He is associated with Customs and GST administration and writes from a practitioner’s perspective on how public institutions function on the ground — covering areas such as revenue protection, regulatory compliance, and career pathways in the public sector. His writing is primarily aimed at students, competitive exam aspirants, and readers interested in understanding the practical side of governance beyond textbooks.
